Ongoing:

Deployment (Ongoing)

  • After signingup and payment workflow at Development for ADT assigning.
  • Access field team deployed link through given ADT and update back office.
  • Access back office team run closure as per given details by field team.

Outdoor Relocation (Ongoing)

  • After applying Relocation and payment, the workflow drop at Development bucket for ADT assigning.
  • Access field team deployed link through given ADT and update back office.
  • Access back office team run closure as per given details by field team.

Migration (Ongoign)

  • Currently the migration process has been performed as per field requirements and new ADT has been allocated, after that NOC runs the migration.

Termination (Ongoing)

  • Termination workflow closes once the device is submitted in the store after acknowledgement of the store person and NMS port also release.
